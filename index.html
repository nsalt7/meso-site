Meso Privacy Policy

Effective 10 July 2026. Meso is built local-first: your training data belongs on your phone, not on our servers. We do not run servers, and we do not have accounts.

What Meso stores, and where

Everything you enter in Meso (workouts, sets, check-ins, notes, settings) is stored on your device. If you enable iCloud, an encrypted backup copy is kept in your personal iCloud key-value storage so your data survives reinstalls and new phones. That copy lives in your Apple account; we cannot read it. You can export or delete all data at any time from the Settings tab.

What leaves your device

Two things, both purchase-related. When you subscribe to Meso Pro, Apple processes the payment; we never see your payment details. To validate subscriptions, the app talks to RevenueCat, which processes a random app-generated identifier and your purchase receipt. This identifier is not linked to your name, email, or training data. RevenueCat's privacy policy is at revenuecat.com/privacy.

What we never collect

No accounts, no email addresses, no analytics on your training, no advertising identifiers, no selling of data. Your workout data is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

Health disclaimer

Meso provides training guidance, not medical advice. Consult a health professional before starting a training program.
